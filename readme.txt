**Ninja Mail is being sunset and no longer functions as of April 1, 2021. We recommend migrating to our new service [SendWP](https://sendwp.com), which sends all of your WordPress transactional email rather than just your Ninja Mail emails.**

== Changelog ==

= 1.0.6 (1 April 2021) =

* Sunset the service.

= 1.0.5 (25 February 2019) =

* Added support for email Reply To.

* Reduced the number of external requests to the server.

*Changes:*
